HAPPY FATHER'S DAY

Celebrating an Extraordinary Father

Dear Daddy,


I don’t think I’ve ever properly said this… but you’re honestly the best. You spoil me more than I deserve 😅, and love me more than I even realize most days. You always try to give me the best—whether it’s things I ask for or even things I never knew I needed. You work so hard,. I seriously don’t know how you do it, but I notice it… even if I don’t say it often. I know I drive you mad sometimes (ok, maybe a lot of times 😄), especially with the solo trip thing. It’s been a whole year and you still haven’t said yes.I keep asking and you keep saying “not now”… (plz say yes as a fathers day gift to me) I also know you have high expectations—especially when it comes to studies.But it’s okay. And yes, I may not be the topper you dream of (yet), but I’m trying… in my own way, at my own pace. Sometimes you’re strict, sometimes you’re soft, sometimes you say things that stay in my head for days, and sometimes you make me laugh when I’m trying to stay mad at you. You’re a mix of everything—and honestly, that’s what makes you best father.
Thank you for all the love, the mini lectures,the concern in your voice when I’m tired, and even the way you silently check on me when I pretend to be okay. I may not always say it, but I do feel lucky to be your daughter. You may not say I love you a lot—but your way of loving is louder than any words. One day I’ll go on that solo trip 😛 and one day you’ll see that I turned out to be how you wanted me to be.
Love you always Stupid Fellow💙
-Your World's best daughter
